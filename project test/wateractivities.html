Ocean City, Maryland is surrounded by water, and you’re surrounded by choices of how to enjoy it! There are great sightseeing tours available that allow you to explore the ecosystem of the ocean and bay via boats, kayaks and catamarans. Or you can hop on water transportation and take in the scenery.

And when you’re ready to venture out on your own, you can rent paddle boards, kayaks, canoes and boats, as well. If it’s thrills you’re looking for, there are jet ski rentals (and lessons!) to get you moving. And let’s not forget the original ocean sport. Ocean City, Maryland offers some of the best surfing in the mid-Atlantic!
